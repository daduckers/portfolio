    • Tiny House Collection

    >> I made these renders for Tiny Foundations, a company in Centerbrook, CT that specializes in building Tiny Houses on wheels and distributing custom Tiny House trailers. I used Blender to model and design the interiors of these houses. I mainly used royalty free models, textures, and HDRI's to complete the project, and modelled custom objects of things I wasn't able to find online for free. I made four of these models, some of which are shown below. If you'd like to see the rest, go check out their website; all of my work is listed there. Because Tiny Foundations is now doing turn-key builds, you can actually buy the Tiny Houses you see below!
